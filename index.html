Privacy Policy

Micra is designed with privacy as a core principle. The app is a local utility tool and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

1. Data Collection

Micra does not collect any personal information, usage statistics, or crash reports. All settings are stored locally on your device using macOS standard defaults.

2. Audio Access

Micra requires access to your microphone solely to perform its primary function: muting and unmuting the device at the system level. Micra does not record, process, or transmit any audio data.

3. Data Transmission

The app has no networking capabilities and does not communicate with any external servers. No data ever leaves your computer.

4. Third Parties

Since no data is collected, no data is ever shared with third parties.

5. Changes to this Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
